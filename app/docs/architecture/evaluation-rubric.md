# InfinityMix Evaluation Rubric

This document defines the human-listening criteria used to evaluate candidate sequence planner variants or analysis engine upgrades against baseline audio generated by InfinityMix.

## Review Process

Use the `scripts/generate-phase2-review-template.mjs` script to generate a JSON scoring template for a specific candidate build. Listen to the output of `scripts/compare-audio-baseline.mjs` against a known baseline, and score each transition on the criteria below.

## Scoring Categories (Scale 1-5)

A score of 1 indicates a critical failure. A score of 5 indicates a perfect, seamless, professional-quality result.

### 1. Phrase Alignment
**How well do the musical phrasing and downbeats align during the transition?**
*   **5 - Perfect:** Seamless phrase snapping. Drops land exactly where expected.
*   **4 - Good:** A slight drift but rhythmically coherent.
*   **3 - Acceptable:** Alignments are mostly correct but occasionally jarring (e.g., missed downbeat by one bar).
*   **2 - Poor:** Frequent trainwrecks; phrases clash noticeably.
*   **1 - Fail:** Complete disregard for the track's musical grid.

### 2. Section Choice Quality (Energy & Flow)
**Did the planner select the right segments to overlap? (e.g., intro to outro, or build to drop)**
*   **5 - Perfect:** Highly dynamic and engaging sequence. Energy builds and releases logically.
*   **4 - Good:** Safe transitioning. Mixes harmoniously but may lack excitement.
*   **3 - Acceptable:** Makes sense but is occasionally confusing (e.g., short vocal clash that isn't fatal).
*   **2 - Poor:** Selects inappropriate sections (clashing verses, double drops).
*   **1 - Fail:** Chaos. No discernible event arc.

### 3. Transition Musicality (Harmonic & Timbral)
**Are there key clashes or bad tempo artifacts? Does the EQ/mix sound clean?**
*   **5 - Perfect:** Keys match perfectly or modulate beautifully. No audible tempo stretching artifacts.
*   **4 - Good:** Slight dissonance that resolves quickly, or minor artifacts.
*   **3 - Acceptable:** Noticeable key clash or pitch shifting, but arguably passable for a casual mix.
*   **2 - Poor:** Painful dissonance. Severe tempo artifacts (chipmunk/Darth Vader effects).
*   **1 - Fail:** Unlistenable. Complete clash of elements.

### 4. Vocal Clash Avoidance (For Mashups)
**If stems are used, is there competition between vocals?**
*   **5 - Perfect:** Crisp acapella over strict instrumental.
*   **4 - Good:** Very minor backing vocal bleed that doesn't distract.
*   **3 - Acceptable:** Some vocal overlap but primary lyric is comprehensible.
*   **2 - Poor:** Two lead vocals compete directly; lyrics are hard to follow.
*   **1 - Fail:** A wall of competing vocal noise.

## Hard Fail Rules
Any dimension receiving a score of **2 or lower** immediately flags the fixture as a regression requiring a fix before rollout. Average scores must improve or remain stable (within 0.5) against the baseline.
