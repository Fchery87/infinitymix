## 1. System Overview

InfinityMix is designed as a microservices-based architecture to ensure scalability, resilience, and maintainable separation of concerns, especially given the compute-intensive nature of audio processing and AI. The system leverages cloud-native technologies for elasticity and managed services for operational efficiency.
